# Desing Tokens

Design tokens in FigTok represent an abstraction of design 
decisions defined within Figma, created to build, maintain, 
and scale design systems consistently.
Elements such as spacing, color, typography, styles, and animations 
are transformed into structured data — in our case, exported 
as JSON, CSS, and list formats.

Instead of relying on hard-coded values, tokens enable visual 
consistency and technical flexibility across all product experiences, 
serving as a direct bridge between design and development.

## Typography

The project uses Inter as its primary typeface, inherited directly 
from Figma’s base typography. Text sizes also follow the recommendations 
outlined in Figma’s official documentation.
